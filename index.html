EVENTS

Exquisite, Extraordinary, Exemplary

Hackathon

With sponsors such as GitHub, AWS Educate and JetBrains among many others, a combined prize money of 80000 and on the spot internships; Hackathon 5.0 was a complete success even after being completely online. Conducted over a span of three days 19th – 21st February 2021, it was a truly awesome experience. But don’t take our word for it, ask the 200+ teams that participated from all over India.

Yugmak

YUGMAK is our annual national convention and is one of the most anticipated events of UPES-CSI consisting of various events all sharing a common theme. The 2021 convention was “A trip down the memory lane” and was conducted over a span of 2 days 17-18 April 2021.

I-HACK

I-HACK is our online cryptic hunt designed to test code breaking and decryption skills of participants. Participants go through levels and at the end of the time limit, the one who clears maximum levels wins.
